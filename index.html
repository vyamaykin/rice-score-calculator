Scoring Guide
Reach — users impacted per quarter
Impact — use scale: 0.25 / 0.5 / 1 / 2 / 3
Confidence — enter as % (e.g. 80)
Effort — person-months to ship
High ≥ 50 — strong candidate, prioritize
Med 20–49 — evaluate tradeoffs carefully
Low < 20 — deprioritize or cut scope
